## Journey 01 — James: UK Company Setting Up a Spanish Subsidiary

**Persona:** James Whitfield (UK SME Owner)
**Pipeline:** LAND + OPERATE
**Duration:** 10–21 days from first contact to engagement letter

### Discovery Phase
1. James searches "how to set up a company in Spain post Brexit UK"
2. Lands on Costa Blanca Bridge blog post or homepage via organic search
3. Reads the homepage — spots "UK & Irish companies post-Brexit" in the Who We Serve section ✓
4. Clicks through to LAND service page
5. Reads the formation process — recognises the complexity he was trying to avoid
6. Sees a case study from a UK firm (similar sector, similar size) ✓
7. Scrolls to CTA — clicks "Book a Discovery Call"

### Consideration Phase
8. 30-minute video call with Armando (or AI-assisted intake form if call not immediately available)
9. Receives a follow-up email within 24 hours with a tailored proposal outline
10. Reviews proposal — asks two clarifying questions via email
11. Receives clear answers within same business day (AI-assisted drafting + human review)

### Decision Phase
12. Receives engagement letter with fixed-fee structure for Phase 1 (formation)
13. Signs engagement letter via DocuSign
14. Receives onboarding checklist and client portal access
15. Formation process begins

### Experience Moments That Matter
- **Moment 1 (Discovery):** Seeing his situation reflected on the homepage. If he doesn't see "UK companies" or "post-Brexit" within 10 seconds, he leaves.
- **Moment 2 (Trust):** The case study. Real outcome, real timeline, real numbers.
- **Moment 3 (Decision):** The fixed-fee proposal. No hourly billing surprises.

### Failure Points to Prevent
- Generic homepage that doesn't mention his situation
- No case study from a comparable company
- Contact form that asks too many questions before he's committed
- Proposal that takes more than 48 hours

## Journey 03 — Mohammed: Establishing an Import SPV for EU Market Access

**Persona:** Mohammed Al-Rashid (Emerging Market Trade Investor)
**Pipeline:** TRADE (Principal Model — Archetype 01)
**Duration:** 2–6 months from first contact to SPV operational

### Discovery Phase
1. Mohammed receives a LinkedIn referral from a mutual contact who has worked with Costa Blanca Bridge
2. Reviews Costa Blanca Bridge's LinkedIn profile and website
3. Reads the "How We Work" / Principal Model page — this is the page that hooks him ✓
4. Reads the Deal Archetypes section — Archetype 01 (Emerging Market Trade Corridors) matches his situation exactly ✓
5. Sends a direct LinkedIn message or email — does NOT fill in a generic contact form

### Consideration Phase
6. Initial email exchange within 24 hours — substantive, demonstrates knowledge of CIS/EM trade structures
7. First call: formal, 45–60 minutes, both sides assess fit
8. Costa Blanca Bridge sends a confidential deal structure outline (NDA first)
9. Second call: deeper — SPV structure, Costa Blanca Bridge equity participation terms, compliance framework
10. Costa Blanca Bridge produces a term sheet for the SPV structure

### Deal Structuring Phase
11. Term sheet reviewed by both parties' legal counsel
12. NDA in place, corporate documentation begins
13. SPV incorporated in Spain with Costa Blanca Bridge holding agreed equity stake
14. Regulatory compliance framework established
15. First goods shipment cleared through the structure

### Experience Moments That Matter
- **Moment 1 (Website):** The Principal Model page. If this page only describes service fees, Mohammed will not contact Costa Blanca Bridge. The co-investment angle must be front and centre.
- **Moment 2 (First Email):** The speed and quality of the response signals whether Costa Blanca Bridge is the right partner.
- **Moment 3 (Term Sheet):** The precision and practicality of the proposed structure. Mohammed has seen many consultants produce vague proposals.

### Failure Points to Prevent
- Website that positions Costa Blanca Bridge as a "legal services provider" without the co-investment angle
- Generic contact form as the only contact option (he won't fill it in)
- Slow or generic first response
- First call that doesn't demonstrate deep knowledge of his deal type

## Cross-Journey Patterns

### Universal Friction Points
1. **Contact form anxiety**: All personas show hesitation with long forms. Solution: single-step "book a call" button as primary CTA; longer form only as alternative.
2. **Trust deficit on first visit**: Every persona is comparing Costa Blanca Bridge with 2–3 alternatives. Solution: social proof (case studies, credentials) within the first scroll of every key page.
3. **Response speed**: Every persona expects a substantive response within 24 hours. Solution: AI-assisted intake and response drafting to ensure consistent, fast, quality replies.
