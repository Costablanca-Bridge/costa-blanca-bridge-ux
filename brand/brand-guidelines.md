## 1. Brand Essence

### What We Are
CostablancaBridge is a **principal bridge** — not an agency, not a consultancy, not a visa service. We walk through the door with our clients. We co-invest in their outcomes. Every visual and verbal decision must reinforce this positioning.

### Brand Personality
- **Authoritative** — We have done this at the highest corporate level. We speak from experience, not theory.
- **Warm** — We are a partner, not a vendor. We are human-scale even when handling institutional deals.
- **Precise** — Legal and financial work demands precision. Our design reflects that. Nothing is sloppy, nothing is vague.
- **Modern** — We are an AI-native company. We move faster than any traditional firm. The brand is contemporary, not stuffy.

### Brand Promise
*"We don't just open the door to Europe. We walk through it with you."*

### Colour Rules

1. **Never** use gold as a background for body text — contrast is insufficient at small sizes
2. **Dark sections** (navy-dark background): use white for headings, blue-gray for body text, gold for accents
3. **Light sections** (cream background): use navy for headings, near-black for body text, gold for accents
4. **CTAs**: Primary CTA is always gold background with navy-dark text. Never invert this.
5. **Minimum contrast ratio**: 4.5:1 for body text (WCAG AA), 3:1 for large text

## 3. Typography

### Typefaces

**Display / Headings:**
```
font-family: 'Playfair Display', Cambria, Georgia, serif;
```
Playfair Display is a sophisticated editorial serif that mirrors the Cambria used in CBB's internal documents. It conveys authority and permanence.

**Body / Interface:**
```
font-family: 'Inter', Calibri, 'Segoe UI', system-ui, sans-serif;
```
Inter is the gold-standard for professional web interfaces. Highly legible, internationally neutral, works at every size.

### Type Scale

| Name | Size | Weight | Line Height | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `display-xl` | 72px / 4.5rem | 700 | 1.1 | Hero headline |
| `display-lg` | 56px / 3.5rem | 700 | 1.15 | Section hero |
| `heading-1` | 40px / 2.5rem | 700 | 1.2 | Page title |
| `heading-2` | 32px / 2rem | 600 | 1.25 | Section heading |
| `heading-3` | 24px / 1.5rem | 600 | 1.3 | Card heading |
| `heading-4` | 20px / 1.25rem | 600 | 1.35 | Sub-section |
| `body-lg` | 18px / 1.125rem | 400 | 1.6 | Lead paragraph |
| `body` | 16px / 1rem | 400 | 1.65 | Standard body |
| `body-sm` | 14px / 0.875rem | 400 | 1.6 | Captions, metadata |
| `label` | 12px / 0.75rem | 600 | 1.4 | Labels, tags (all-caps + letter-spacing: 0.08em) |

### Typography Rules

1. **Headlines are Playfair Display** — always. No exceptions.
2. **Body copy is Inter** — always. No exceptions.
3. **Maximum line length**: 70 characters for body text. Content wider than this uses columns or max-width constraints.
4. **Hero headlines**: Use italic Playfair Display for the second line (creates visual contrast with upright first line)
5. **No underlining** for non-links. Use weight or colour for emphasis.
6. **Sentence case** for headings (not title case) except for proper nouns and brand names.

## 4. Logo

### Primary Logo
The CBB bridge icon with "Costa Blanca Bridge" wordmark and "Connecting Worlds" sub-tagline. Gold bridge on navy background.

**File:** `IMG_2676 5.JPG` (in the root Costa Blanca Bridge folder)

### Logo Usage Rules

1. **Minimum size**: 120px wide for digital, never smaller
2. **Clear space**: maintain a margin equal to the height of the "C" in "Costa" on all sides
3. **On dark backgrounds**: use the logo as-is (gold on navy)
4. **On light backgrounds**: use the logo with a navy background box, or reverse to navy bridge on white
5. **Never** distort, rotate, recolour, or apply effects to the logo
6. **Never** place the logo on the gold colour — insufficient contrast

### Logo Variations Needed (Future)
- Horizontal lockup (icon + wordmark side by side)
- Icon-only mark (for favicons, social avatars)
- White version (for dark backgrounds without the navy box)
- One-colour version (for embossed/print applications)

## 5. Spacing & Layout

### Spacing Scale (base: 4px)
```
4px, 8px, 12px, 16px, 24px, 32px, 40px, 48px, 64px, 80px, 96px, 128px, 160px, 192px
```

### Grid
- **Desktop**: 12-column grid, 80px gutters, max-width 1280px container
- **Tablet**: 8-column grid, 48px gutters
- **Mobile**: 4-column grid, 24px gutters, 16px side margins

### Section Rhythm
- **Small sections**: 80px padding top/bottom
- **Standard sections**: 120px padding top/bottom
- **Hero sections**: 160px padding top/bottom
- **Between sections on same background**: reduce padding by 50%

## 6. Visual Style

### Photography Direction
When sourcing imagery (future):
- **Architecture and urban environments**: modern European cityscapes, Costa Blanca sea views, financial district buildings
- **People**: professional, diverse, natural (not stock-photo staged). Real business interactions.
- **Mood**: warm, golden light where possible — connects to the brand colour palette
- **No clichés**: no handshakes, no globe graphics, no generic "business meeting" photos

### Iconography
- Style: line icons, 2px stroke, rounded caps
- Size: 20px (inline), 24px (card icons), 48px (feature icons)
- Colour: gold on dark backgrounds, navy on light backgrounds
- Library reference: Lucide Icons (matches the line style)

### Cards and Surfaces
- **Primary card**: white background, 1px `#E2DDD4` border, `border-radius: 12px`, subtle drop shadow
- **Dark card**: navy-mid background, 1px gold-20%-opacity border
- **Gold accent cards**: navy-dark background, 3px left border in gold
- **Section dividers**: gold horizontal rule, 2px height, partial width (not full bleed)

### Motion & Animation
- Transition duration: `200ms` for hover states, `300ms` for reveals
- Easing: `cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)` (material-style ease)
- Reveal animations: fade up, `translateY(20px)` → `translateY(0)` with opacity 0→1
- No decorative animations that delay content

## 7. Voice & Tone

### Core Principles
1. **Confident, not arrogant** — We state what we do, we don't oversell it
2. **Precise, not cold** — Every word earns its place, but we are still human
3. **Direct, not blunt** — Short sentences, active voice, but with warmth
4. **Expert, not jargon-heavy** — We know the law and the deal structures, but we explain them clearly

### Vocabulary Guidance

**Use:**
- "We walk through it with you"
- "Your European chapter begins here"
- "We cross the bridge with you"
- "Structured for success"
- "Principal partner"
- "Your legal architecture"

**Avoid:**
- "Synergy", "leverage", "ecosystem" (generic consulting language)
- "One-stop shop" (sounds transactional)
- "We are passionate about..." (empty)
- "Industry-leading" without evidence
- "Seamless" — overused
- Excessive legal jargon without explanation

### CTA Copy
- Primary: **"Book a Discovery Call"** (not "Contact Us", not "Get Started")
- Secondary: **"Explore Our Services"**
- Newsletter/early access: **"Join the Waitlist"** or **"Get Early Access"**
- Deal-focused: **"Discuss a Deal"**

## 8. Don'ts

| ❌ Don't | ✓ Instead |
|---|---|
| Use gold as a large background | Use navy or cream as section background |
| Use more than 2 typefaces | Playfair Display + Inter only |
| Crowd content into tight spaces | Use the spacing scale, breathe |
| Use generic stock photography | Source real, warm, architectural imagery |
| Treat every page identically | Hero sections vary; create visual rhythm |
| Underline non-link text | Use bold or colour change for emphasis |
| Write headlines in ALL CAPS | Use sentence case; all-caps only for labels |
| Use the logo on gold | Navy or white backgrounds only |
